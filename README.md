# 🌿 Inner Mirror

**Inner Mirror** is an AI-powered reflective journaling application that helps users better understand the patterns of their own minds. Rather than simply storing journal entries, the application analyzes them over time to identify recurring emotions, behavioral tendencies, hidden sources of stress, strengths, and long-term personal growth.

The project is inspired by a remarkably detailed framework for understanding the human mind and human experience that was developed over 2,500 years ago. Instead of presenting ancient terminology or religious concepts, Inner Mirror translates these timeless insights into simple, compassionate, and practical reflections that are accessible to anyone, regardless of their background or beliefs.

## Why Inner Mirror?

Most journaling applications focus on recording events.

Inner Mirror focuses on understanding the person experiencing those events.

Every day, our thoughts, emotions, fears, expectations, habits, and reactions quietly shape our decisions, relationships, and overall well-being. Yet many of these patterns remain unnoticed because we rarely step back to observe them over time.

Inner Mirror acts as a private mirror for the mind. It helps users recognize recurring emotional and behavioral patterns across weeks and months, encouraging greater self-awareness and thoughtful reflection.

The goal is not to judge, diagnose, or advise users. Instead, the application provides a calm and supportive space for observing how the mind responds to everyday life.

## Key Features

* ✍️ **AI-powered journal analysis** that goes beyond summarizing entries.
* 🧠 **Long-term pattern recognition** across multiple journal entries.
* 📈 **Awareness, Clarity, Equanimity, and Reflection** metrics to visualize personal growth over time.
* 🔍 Identification of recurring emotional patterns such as stress, fear, comparison, attachment, anger, guilt, and validation-seeking.
* 🌱 Recognition of positive qualities including patience, compassion, honesty, resilience, and self-awareness.
* 💬 Gentle reflection prompts and self-inquiry questions to encourage deeper understanding.
* 📊 Interactive dashboards showing emotional trends and recurring themes.
* 🔄 Re-analysis of previous journal entries as the analysis engine evolves.
* 🔒 Privacy-first architecture with local data storage, ensuring journal entries remain under the user's control.

## Who Is It For?

Inner Mirror is designed for anyone interested in understanding themselves more deeply, including:

* Individuals who maintain a journaling practice
* Professionals navigating stress and burnout
* Parents and caregivers seeking emotional support
* People interested in personal growth and self-reflection
* Coaches and therapists exploring reflective tools
* Anyone curious about recurring mental and emotional patterns

## Design Philosophy

The application emphasizes simplicity, psychological safety, and calm reflection.

The interface uses a minimalist design with soft pastel colors to create a warm, welcoming environment that encourages honest self-expression. Every design decision is intended to reduce cognitive overload and make reflection feel natural rather than overwhelming.

## Guiding Principles

Inner Mirror is intentionally designed to be:

* Compassionate rather than judgmental
* Reflective rather than prescriptive
* Accessible rather than academic
* Private rather than intrusive
* Insightful rather than overwhelming

It is **not** intended to replace therapy, mental health professionals, or medical advice. Instead, it serves as a personal reflection companion that helps users better understand their own thoughts, emotions, and behaviors.

## Technology Stack

* **Frontend:** React + Vite
* **Charts:** Recharts
* **AI Analysis:** Groq API
* **Storage:** Browser Local Storage
* **Deployment:** Vercel

## Vision

The long-term vision of Inner Mirror is to make deep self-understanding more accessible through thoughtful use of AI and reflective journaling.

By helping users notice patterns that often remain hidden in daily life, Inner Mirror aims to foster greater emotional awareness, healthier relationships, wiser decision-making, and a more balanced way of living—one journal entry at a time.
